About Us

Who we are...

We are two Ipswich/Brisbane based mummas & friends (Jacq & Katie), bringing their love of indoor plants and crafting together, creating Moore Weir'd knots & pots!

How it all started...

Katie was the first (as usual), to plant the seed (no pun intendedðŸ˜‰) and idea into Jacq's head about selling her hand painted pots in a part time capacity. As it was Katie's idea, Jacq is dragging Katie along for the ride!

So, what do we do?

Jacqui paints the funky pots, and Katie makes the gorgeous macrame hangers. All of the colour cord used is 100% recycled cotton, dyed and produced in an evironmentally concious factory, that practices Ethical Trade. The colours have been sealed on our pots to help against fading or discolouration.

The why... 

Our hobby is 'Adding Moore Weir'd to your home through our funky, one of a kind, vibrantly coloured pots and knots. Offering more colour options to you and your indoor jungle, at an affordable price!
